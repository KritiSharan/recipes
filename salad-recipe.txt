Ingredients:
 - 1 muskmelon
 - half a watermelon
 - 2 tablespoons pepper powder
 - 1 teaspoon black salt
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon orange juice
 - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
 - 5-7 mint leaves

Directions:
 1. Scoop out balls of melon and set aside to refrigerate for 10 min
 2. Take out the melon balls and add the juices
 3. Sprinkle both salts and pepper powder
 4. Garnish with mint leaves
 5. Serve chilled
